Who We Are

4Vagos is a dedicated team of beekeepers located on Peach Ridge Rd in Brookshire, TX. We proudly produce local, organic golden honey while supporting a sustainable ecosystem. We believe in nurturing our bees and working harmoniously with nature to bring the best quality honey to your table.

What We Do

We specialize in ethical beekeeping practices, ensuring the health of our bees while providing pure, raw honey. Our honey is harvested locally, using environmentally-friendly methods that protect the bees and preserve the quality of the honey.

What Is Our Passion

Our passion is simple: caring for our bees and producing honey that is as pure as nature intended. We are committed to the craft of beekeeping and to educating our community about the importance of bees in maintaining biodiversity. Every jar of our organic golden honey is a testament to our love for bees and our dedication to quality.
